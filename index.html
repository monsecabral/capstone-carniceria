Welcome!

Monse's Super Carniceria #1 is our first online store in a chain of carnicerias dedicated to bringing authentic Mexican ingredients right to your door.

We currently offer a few select items that best represent Mexican cuisine such as:

  • avocados
  • tomatoes
  • and more!

These ingredients have been consumed by the indigenous people of Mexico and portions of North America for thousands of years.

We hope you enjoy them as much as we have!
